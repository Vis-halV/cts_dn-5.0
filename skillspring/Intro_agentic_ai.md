## Understanding Agentic AI

### What is Agentic AI?

- Adapt to new information and environments
- Make decisions based on defined goals

### Examples 

- Autonomous vehicles
- Virtual assistants
- Manufacturing robots

### Benefits

- Improve task efficiency
- Reduce human oversight
- Enhance decision-making processes
